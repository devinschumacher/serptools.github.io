Software Overview

IMSI TurboPDF is an application used to produce high quality PDF documents. The program can view, edit, convert, scan, and encrypt PDF files.

TurboPDF provides a variety of editing capabilities, which includes changing text, adding and deleting pages, designing forms, and inserting headers, footers, images, and video into your document. The program features OCR technology that allows you to convert image-based or scanned content into selectable text. You can convert and export PDF files to other formats, including Microsoft Office XML, HTML, plain text, JPEG, and PNG.

The application enables you to markup and add comments to PDF documents, which is helpful when working collaboratively. The utility also comes with security features, such as password encryption, digital signatures, and certificate encryption, for safeguarding your documents.

TurboPDF is a handy utility if you consistently work with PDF files. The program can create, view, edit, convert, scan, and encrypt PDF documents. Also, the price of IMSI TurboPDF is rather low in comparison to other PDF applications, most notably Adobe Acrobat, which makes it an attractive solution to your PDF needs.
