  • Integrates with bluetooth headsets and AirPlay

Software Overview

VideoLAN VLC for iOS is a port of the popular VLC media player to iPhone, iPad, and iTouch. The app can be modified under the Mozilla Public License and GNU General Public License.

VLC for iOS supports all the same audio and video formats supported by VLC media player and includes support for network streams such as MMS, HLS, and RTSP. The app comes with video filters, fine seeking, and playback speed manipulation. You can also synchronize media through iTunes, iCloud, Dropbox, OneDrive, Google Drive, and WiFi sharing.

This app provides a mobile version of the beloved VLC media player users have come to favor for video and audio playback on their Windows and OS X machines. It provides the same format support as well as some useful features such as filters and playback speed manipulation. If you enjoy VLC media player you'll enjoy the VideoLAN VLC for iOS app for playing media on your iOS device.
