Software Overview

ShedWorx Smart Converter is a lightweight video and audio converter for Windows and OS X. The application is available for download as the Pro version or the free demo version.

The application supports a variety of media formats, such as .MOV, .WMV, .WMA, and .AVI. Smart Converter makes it simple and easy for you to use, you can drag and drop your files onto the application and choose the conversion format. The application detects only what needs to be converted in the file and leaves any other data alone, which decreases conversion time and maintains the highest quality of video and audio.

Smart Converter is a fast video converter with a simple, easy-to-use interface. However, it doesn't offer much more than video and audio conversion. If you are only looking for a program to quickly convert your video or audio to one of the supported formats, ShedWorx Smart Converter is a great option.
