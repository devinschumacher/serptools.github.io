Software Overview

MAGIX Photostory Deluxe is a Windows application for creating customized slideshows from pictures and video clips. It supports many multimedia file types, allowing you to edit images, apply various effects, share slideshows in other formats, upload them online, and burn them to physical media. The program replaced PhotoStory on CD & DVD Deluxe and is only available for Windows.

The application supports various media formats, including .JPG, .PNG, .MP3, .MPG, and .WMV, which you can import into your project. The application also supports many camera RAW formats, including .CR2, .ARW, and .DNG. You can alter these images' color, brightness, contrast, and saturation with the editing tools provided by Photostory Deluxe.

The program features over 300 cinema-quality special effects and transitions, 850 sounds and songs, and 130 animation templates for bringing your presentations to life. You can animate travel routes, create 3D effects, and apply professional image filters. After editing your slideshow, you can export your project to various formats, burn it to a DVD, or share it to your TV, a mobile device, or the web.

Summary

Photostory Deluxe is geared towards home users looking to organize and present their images and videos. The application supports many popular media formats and provides various artistic features that allow you to customize your presentation. For Windows users looking to create lasting visual memories from their images, video, and audio, try MAGIX Photostory Deluxe.
