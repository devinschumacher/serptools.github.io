Software Overview

Adobe DNG Converter is a free utility that photographers use to convert camera RAW files to the Digital Negative (.DNG) image format. Photographers can then open their converted images in any of the many image viewers and editors that support the DNG format.

DNG Converter allows users to convert camera RAW files from over 600 types of digital cameras, including Fujifilm, Panasonic, Sony, Nikon, Olympus, Samsung, and Ricoh cameras. The program's interface is straightforward and understandable, making it easy for photographers to import and convert photos. If necessary, photographers can modify a handful of conversion settings, including converted images' camera RAW compatibility, JPEG preview size, and compression type.

Summary

DNG Converter does only one thing, but it does that one thing well. If you need to convert your camera RAW files to the DNG format, Adobe DNG Converter will get the job done.
