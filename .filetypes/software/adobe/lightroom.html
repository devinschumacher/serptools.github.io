Main Features

  • Non-destructive editing that preserves your original photos while allowing you to make edits
  • AI-powered tools, such as AI-based subject selection, sky masking, and denoise
  • Cloud syncing and cross-device access for editing images anytime, anywhere
  • Organizational tools like albums, star ratings, flags, keywords, and metadata filtering to manage large photo libraries
  • Built-in and downloadable presets and adaptive presets
  • Export photos in various formats and share them directly to social media

Software Overview

Adobe Lightroom is a photo editing and organizing app for photographers of all skill levels. The program offers numerous image-editing features, support for various file types, and cloud integration. It is available for Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, and the web, making it easy to access and edit photos from nearly any device. Adobe also offers a desktop-only version called Lightroom Classic, which provides a more advanced workflow for seasoned professionals who prefer a local file system over cloud storage.

Lightroom provides features that help you easily import, organize, and enhance your photos. You can sort images using ratings, flags, albums, and metadata, then apply edits ranging from simple adjustments like cropping and exposure correction to advanced tools like masking with AI-powered subject detection, lens corrections, tone curves, split toning, and the Color Mixer. The app also includes hundreds of presets, including adaptive presets that automatically adjust to your photo's subject and lighting conditions.

Cloud integration is a key feature of Lightroom. All edits are saved automatically and synced across your devices through Adobe Creative Cloud, so you can start editing an image on your desktop and then continue on your phone or tablet. You can also export photos to several formats, including .JPG, .TIF, .JXL, and .DNG, and share them directly on social media or via albums.

New in recent updates, Adobe has added tools to Lightroom to help both casual users and experienced photographers get results faster and with less manual effort. For example, the app includes AI-powered denoise, HDR support, editing history, and adaptive presets for portraits, skies, and subjects, further streamlining the editing process while maintaining professional-level control.

Summary

Lightroom is a user-friendly yet feature-rich photo editing app. It offers tools that scale from beginner to pro, support for many file types, and seamless syncing across devices, making Adobe Lightroom an excellent choice for enhancing, organizing, and sharing your photography from anywhere.
