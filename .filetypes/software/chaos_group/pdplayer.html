Software Overview

Chaos Group Pdplayer is an image sequence player and viewer. It is designed to be used by professionals in the 3D, CG, and VFX industries.

Pdplayer is used to combine images and video to create multi-layered sequences. The program supports a variety of industry standard formats such as DP, HDR, EXR, R3D, TGA, CIN, and VRIMG. Pdplayer offers a variety of features including layered sequence playback for unlimited layers, realtime color corrections, and on-set grading and keying.

The program provides 2K+ playback of images and video that is only limited by the power of the CPU or speed of the storage. The application also comes with support for checkerboard, anaglyph, and interlaced stereoscopic playback and can detect stereoscopic sequences when adding a layer.

Even though Pdplayer is a lightweight application, it boasts a large amount of helpful features. It supports many of the industry standard formats, provides 2K+ playback, realtime color correction, and stereoscopic playback. Chaos Group Pdplayer is a quality choice for professionals in the visual effects industry.
